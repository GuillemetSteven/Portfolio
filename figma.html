Introduction to the GUB Project

Discover how our carpooling app is revolutionizing urban mobility.

Introduction

  • Team presentation: We were four during the project to create a real friendly experience
  • Explanation of the logo and name: GUB - Our company is like a chameleon, able to adapt and thrive in any situation.
  • Brief description of the application: "GUB simplifies carpooling and aims to combat student loneliness while reducing the carbon footprint."

GUB Context

  • What is the project? Development of the carpooling concept for shared and eco-responsible trips.
  • Identification of the need: Born from a market study and personal observations on traffic jams and pollution.
  • Technology and innovation: Use of a Tinder-type matching algorithm to connect drivers and passengers.

Future Vision

  • The problem and the solution: GUB reduces greenhouse gases and encourages trip sharing, strengthening the social fabric.
  • Growth potential: GUB is scalable and can be integrated with other services, with features like carpooling for special events.

Conclusion

  • Recap: GUB is an innovative carpooling solution that uses a matching algorithm to pair riders with drivers based on their preferences and needs, making for a more comfortable and sustainable urban mobility experience.
  • Next steps: We are working on partnerships and the continuous improvement of the application.

Thinking Process and Maquette Creation

We used Figma to design and test our maquettes, incorporating user feedback in each iteration.

Presentation to a Jury

We presented our project to a jury and were proud to receive first place and a letter of recommendation. This experience has strengthened our resilience and our ability to evolve.

Alternative Solution and Positive Impact

GUB is an innovative alternative to traditional modes of transportation, with a significant positive impact on the environment and the urban community.
